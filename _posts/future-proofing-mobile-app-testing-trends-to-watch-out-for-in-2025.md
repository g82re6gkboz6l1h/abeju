## The Evolution of Mobile App Testing

The complexity of modern apps demands robust testing solutions. Traditional manual testing is being replaced by AI and automated tools that enhance scalability and precision within agile development frameworks. Factors shaping this evolution include:

1. **Increased device fragmentation** with diverse operating systems and screen sizes.
2. **Emerging technologies** like AI, blockchain, and AR demanding advanced testing strategies.
3. A push for **seamless, user-centric experiences** across platforms.

Tackling Device Compatibility by 2025

With the rise of foldable screens, wearables, and IoT devices, ensuring consistency across devices is crucial. Testing will need to address:

- **Screen rescaling** for foldable and high-res displays.
- **Hardware diversity** and integration with various sensors.
- **Peripheral testing** for IoT and wearable tech.

AI tools like Zof AI will lead efforts in automating compatibility checks and minimizing errors.
