## Overcoming Traditional Mobile App Testing Challenges

### 1. **Device Fragmentation**
Testing across the vast array of devices, operating systems, and screen resolutions is a significant challenge, which traditional methods struggle to address.

### 2. **Short Development Cycles**
Agile practices and DevOps pipelines often leave insufficient time for thorough manual testing, increasing the risk of bugs.

### 3. **Costly Manual Testing**
Scaling manual testing across multiple devices and scenarios is expensive and time-intensive.

### 4. **User Behavior Complexity**
Traditional methods fail to replicate real-world user behavior effectively, limiting test accuracy.

### 5. **Dynamic Updates**
Frequent updates require tests to adapt rapidly to ensure app compatibility and performance.
