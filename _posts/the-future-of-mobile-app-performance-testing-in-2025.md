To align with emerging technologies, testing must prioritize metrics like network latency (optimized for 5G), load testing under high user traffic, battery efficiency evaluations, edge computing responsiveness, and enhanced user experience benchmarks. The widespread adoption of 5G and edge computing further elevates app performance expectations. AI-based testing solutions streamline simulations and ensure apps meet the high-speed, decentralized requirements of these technologies.
