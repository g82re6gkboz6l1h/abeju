## 2. Ensure Cross-Platform Compatibility

### Challenge:
Apps must function correctly across platforms (iOS, Android, etc.) and varying screen sizes while new devices are launched frequently.

**Solution:**
1. Leverage cost-saving device emulators for early testing.
2. Conduct real device tests for authentic results.
3. Use advanced automation tools, such as Appium or Selenium, to streamline the process.
