### Tackling Multi-Device and OS Fragmentation

From IoT devices to foldable screens and augmented reality platforms, the diversity of devices in 2025 complicates app testing. Ensuring compatibility across thousands of Android devices and ever-evolving iOS versions requires robust strategies.

#### Action Steps:
- **Embrace Device-Cloud Testing**: Access virtual device environments for seamless testing across different configurations.
- **Prioritize User Bases**: Focus testing on high-priority devices and OS versions for optimal impact.
