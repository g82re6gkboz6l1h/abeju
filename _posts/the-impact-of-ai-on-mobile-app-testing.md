### Challenges in Traditional Mobile Testing

Traditional QA testing faces significant hurdles that impede accurate and efficient processes:

1. **Device Fragmentation**: Hundreds of devices, operating system versions, screen sizes, and hardware specs create a fragmented testing environment.
2. **Manual Testing Limitations**: Manual efforts are prone to human error and are time-consuming.
3. **Dynamic User Behavior**: Complex app features often restrict testers from predicting diverse user journeys effectively.
4. **Scaling Complexity**: Traditional testing methods struggle with the growing complexity and scope of apps.
5. **Time Constraints**: Shrinking development cycles demand faster, yet thorough testing.

AI solutions eradicate these barriers, expediting processes, enhancing accuracy, and predicting potential issues.

4. **Cost Optimization**: By minimizing repetitive manual tasks, AI reduces operational costs.
