## What Is Continuous Testing?

Continuous Testing integrates automated testing into every phase of the software development lifecycle (SDLC), significantly reducing time to market and elevating app performance. As mobile apps experience frequent updates, Continuous Testing ensures consistent performance and early detection of bugs. It works hand-in-hand with Agile methodologies, offering seamless, accelerated feedback loops to keep development on track.
