## 2. Overcoming Cross-Platform Testing Challenges

In 2025, apps will need flawless functionality across iOS, Android, web browsers, IoT devices, and more. This diversification introduces complex challenges for testers:

### Device Fragmentation
The ever-increasing array of device types with unique configurations and dependencies will challenge consistent testing. For instance, testing a single fitness app on a smartwatch, smartphone, and AR headset demands rigorous evaluation to ensure consistency.

### Evolving User Interfaces (UI)
As interfaces favor AR/VR environments, apps must deliver an optimal user experience across highly varied platforms and technologies.

### Compatibility Testing
Backward and forward compatibility will grow more challenging as operating system updates accelerate, and new devices are released frequently.
