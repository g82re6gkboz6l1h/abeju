Tackling Compatibility Across Next-Gen Devices

The device ecosystem of 2025 will encompass foldable tech, wearables, and AR platforms. Testing strategies must evolve to cater to dynamic interfaces and frequent system updates.

**Challenges and Solutions:**

- **Foldable Screens**: Test apps for adaptive layout transitions between modes—folded, expanded, and multi-screen.
- **Wearables**: Assess usability in compact digital spaces, optimizing interface precision and efficiency.
- **OS Updates**: Prioritize forward compatibility for current and future installations.
- **Virtual Device Platforms**: Leverage cloud ecosystems to simulate hundreds of configurations without requiring excessive hardware setups.

Future-ready compatibility ensures apps remain resilient amid tech innovation and competitive pressure.
