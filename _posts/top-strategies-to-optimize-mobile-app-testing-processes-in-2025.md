Implementing Shift-Left Testing for Early Detection

Shift-left testing integrates QA into the development phase, reducing reworks and delays during app production.

### Advantages of Shift-Left:
1. **Quick Error Detection:** Resolve errors during their discovery phase, improving precision.
2. **Streamlined Workflow:** Fewer iterations improve testing agility.
3. **Supports DevOps & CI/CD:** Compatible for continuous improvements.
