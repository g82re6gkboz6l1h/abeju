## Why Cross-Platform Testing Is a Must-Have in 2025

The growing dominance of cross-platform apps demands rigorous testing. Here's why:

### 1. **Complex Device Ecosystem**
A global variety of devices means developers must ensure compatibility across thousands of screen resolutions and OS versions.

### 2. **High Consumer Expectations**
Modern users demand error-free performance and seamless experiences across all devices.

### 3. **Cost Efficiency**
Cross-platform frameworks like Flutter and React Native reduce development time, but testing remains a key factor for quality assurance.

### 4. **Competitive Market**
Dynamic app environments require continuous updates and bug-free performance to retain users and outpace competitors.
