Top QA Strategies Expected to Shape 2025

Modern challenges demand refined strategies. These methods will dominate:

### Shift-Left Development 
Testing early in the lifecycle, integrated into CI/CD workflows, saves time and reduces costly late-stage errors.

### User-Centric Behavior Testing 
Simulating real-world scenarios like network limitations ensures robust app functionality under diverse conditions.

### Comprehensive Cross-Platform Testing 
Automating cross-platform and hybrid framework testing will become integral with frameworks like Flutter growing in popularity.

### Security-Focused QA 
Data breaches demand security-first strategies with penetration testing and AI threat analysis becoming key standards.
