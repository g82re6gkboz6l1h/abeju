## Tackling Cross-Platform Compatibility 
With platforms like iOS, Android, AR/VR, and smart TVs, testers must ensure seamless performance across diverse devices in 2025.

### Top Challenges:

- **Device Fragmentation:** Compatibility testing across thousands of Android models and OS versions.
- **Platform-Specific Features:** Adapting to unique upgrades for each OS.
- **UI Consistency:** Maintaining uniform UX/UI on different devices and screen resolutions.

### Solutions:
- Leverage cross-platform testing frameworks like Appium or Flutter, alongside AI-driven tools like **Zof AI**, to automate compatibility checks and enhance UI uniformity.
- Use device farms to replicate varied testing environments.
