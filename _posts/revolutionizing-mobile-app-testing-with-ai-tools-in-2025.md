1. **Hyper-Automation**: Intelligent platforms will handle regression, smoke testing, and other repetitive processes.
2. **Real-Time Bug Detection**: Predictive capabilities will identify and resolve bugs preemptively.
3. **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: AI tools will adapt test cases for Android, iOS, and other ecosystems.
4. **AI-IoT Interoperability**: Seamless testing between mobile apps and IoT hardware will become essential.
5. **Performance Insights**: Machine learning will monitor app performance under diverse conditions.
6. **Enhanced Security**: AI will identify vulnerabilities to improve app security.
