Preparing for Emerging Technologies

### Impact of 5G Networks
- 5G creates low-latency applications, which demand testing frameworks capable of analyzing high-speed, real-time networking environments.

### AR/VR Testing Challenges
- AI-powered platforms simulate scenarios related to spatial interactions, UI/UX consistency, and hardware compatibility for AR/VR applications.

### Blockchain-based Apps
- Decentralized apps require specific tests for smart contracts, cryptographic protocols, and data security. Cloud-based simulation aids in accuracy and scalability.
