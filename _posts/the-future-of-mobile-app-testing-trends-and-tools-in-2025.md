## Key Challenges in Mobile App Testing

### Device Fragmentation
The number of unique devices and platforms continues to grow. Testing apps across all configurations is becoming increasingly difficult.

### Time Constraints
Business needs demand faster app development cycles, pushing QA teams to maintain quality despite reduced timelines.

### Real-World Data Simulation
Handling low connectivity, battery drain, and varied geolocations remains challenging in traditional testing.

### Security at Scale
The escalation of cyberattacks on mobile apps calls for robust security testing practices to eliminate vulnerabilities.
