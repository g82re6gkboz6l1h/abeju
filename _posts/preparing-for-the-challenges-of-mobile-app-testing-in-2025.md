### Prioritizing Accessibility in Future Testing

Accessibility testing will be paramount by 2025 as inclusivity takes center stage, ensuring apps support all users, including people with disabilities or technological barriers. Compliance with global standards like WCAG will become a critical benchmark for success.

**Key Accessibility Testing Practices:**
- Compatibility with assistive technologies like screen readers and voice commands.
- Testing for AR/IoT apps with inclusive design that supports various sensory and physical abilities.
- Collaboration with diverse audiences to refine usability for everyone.
