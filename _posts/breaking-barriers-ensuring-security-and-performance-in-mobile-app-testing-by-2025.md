## Key Challenges in Modern Mobile App Security

### Evolving Cybersecurity Threats

Sophisticated attacks exploit app vulnerabilities, including malware, ransomware, and phishing. By 2025, advancements like AI-driven functionality and IoT will expand attack surfaces, requiring enhanced security measures.

### Dependency on Third-Party Services

Reliance on APIs, cloud services, and libraries increases security risks. Third-party vulnerabilities could jeopardize an app's ecosystem, exposing sensitive data.

### Meeting Stringent Regulations

Laws like GDPR and CCPA demand high security standards. With new regulations emerging globally, developers must navigate complex compliance requirements in 2025.
