## Key Mobile App Testing Challenges in 2025

### 1. **Device and OS Fragmentation**
The proliferation of smartphones, foldable screens, wearables, and IoT devices presents challenges in app optimization for diverse environments and platforms.

### 2. **Speed vs. Quality**
Agile and DevOps models demand faster releases, often risking app quality due to tight deadlines.

### 3. **Security Risks**
Cyberattacks are increasing. QA workflows must incorporate advanced security checks to safeguard sensitive user data.

### 4. **Scalability Needs**
Mobile apps must perform flawlessly for millions of users, requiring resilient scalability testing.

### 5. **Cross-Platform Compatibility**
Supporting Android, iOS, and hybrid frameworks demands robust multi-platform testing.

### 6. **AI Integration Testing**
AI-driven applications need holistic tests for accuracy, personalized analytics, and unbiased results.
