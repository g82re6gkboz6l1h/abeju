## Key Features for Selecting Mobile Testing Software

Ensure your app testing tool includes these essential features:

1. **Multi-Platform Testing** – Covers Android, iOS, and hybrid frameworks.
2. **Automation** – Reduces time spent on repetitive tests via scripting or AI-driven functionality.
3. **Real-Device Testing** – Validates app behavior on actual hardware.
4. **Performance Analytics** – Measures the app’s speed and stability during heavy usage.
5. **CI/CD Integration** – Allows seamless testing during development stages.
6. **AI Capabilities** – Predicts issues and improves testing efficiency, like Zof AI.
7. **Usability** – Easy-to-navigate interface for testers at any expertise level.
