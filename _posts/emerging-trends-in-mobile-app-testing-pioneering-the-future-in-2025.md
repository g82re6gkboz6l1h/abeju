## Mobile App Testing Evolution: From Manual to AI-powered

In its early years, mobile app testing relied on manual processes, which were time-intensive and prone to error. The advent of automation tools such as Selenium and Appium transformed the landscape, enabling faster, more accurate testing of functionality, performance, usability, and security. The integration of machine learning and AI by the mid-2020s further optimized mobile app testing with predictive analytics and automated workflows. By 2025, agile workflows, AI-powered solutions, and cross-device compatibility have become the norm.
