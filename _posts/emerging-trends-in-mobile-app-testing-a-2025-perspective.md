Rise of Automation in Mobile App Testing

Automation is now essential for handling complex and fast-paced app environments. Tools like Selenium and Appium streamline testing processes with the following benefits:

1. **Speed**: Enables quick error detection and resolution.
2. **Consistency**: Eliminates human bias and error.
3. **Cost Savings**: Reduces manual tasks, cutting costs over time.

Integrated with CI/CD workflows, automated testing supports continuous delivery, improving deployment efficiency in today's competitive markets.
