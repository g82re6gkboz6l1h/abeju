## Why Mobile Testing Needs a Makeover by 2025

Advancements in mobile technology and evolving user needs necessitate a dynamic shift in testing workflows. Here's why testing practices need to adapt to future demands:

1. Rapid Release Cycles: CI/CD pipelines are accelerating time-to-market while requiring higher accuracy in testing.
2. Complex Applications: Apps integrating AR, IoT, and biometric systems require rigorous and multifaceted testing.
3. AI Integration: Testing for bias, fairness, and machine learning accuracy is vital as AI features become standard.
4. Platform Diversity: Android, iOS, and emerging platforms demand tailored testing processes to ensure compatibility.

Testing teams can leverage modern technologies, including AI, to meet these evolving challenges with agility and scalability.
