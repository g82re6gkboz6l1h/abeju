The diverse and expanding range of devices, wearables, and IoT technologies requires robust device compatibility testing. In 2025, developers can follow these strategies to eliminate compatibility issues:

### Best Practices for Compatibility Testing:
1. **Tap Into Cloud-Based Testing Tools** to access a wide array of virtual and real devices without the overhead of in-house resources. AI solutions like Zof AI further improve efficiency in testing scenarios.
2. **Invest in Real-Device Testing** for identifying nuanced issues on emerging hardware like foldable phones and wearables.
3. **Automate OS- and Hardware-Specific Test Updates** to stay current with frequent updates launched by manufacturers.
4. **Test for 5G Optimization** ensuring apps perform well under high-speed, low-latency networks necessary for AR/VR and similar high-bandwidth user experiences.
