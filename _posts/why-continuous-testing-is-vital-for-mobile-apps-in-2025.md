## What is Continuous Testing?

Continuous testing ensures uninterrupted quality by executing automated tests throughout the software development lifecycle (**SDLC**)—validating every code change immediately. Unlike traditional QA methods, where testing occurs post-major milestones, CT fosters early detection of bugs, security lapses, and compatibility issues.

### Why is Continuous Testing Critical for Mobile Apps?

- **Cross-Platform Compatibility**: Validate performance on Android, iOS, and diverse device versions in real-time.
- **App Store Compliance**: Adhere to rigorous Apple and Google Play guidelines to prevent rejection.
- **Consumer Expectations**: Minimize app crashes and optimize user experience to retain trust and avoid negative reviews.

CT shields developers against disastrous user churn or performance setbacks—helping ensure consistent app delivery standards.
