Cloud-based testing is set to dominate by 2025 with its flexibility, cost-efficiency, and scalability:

- Enables testing across diverse devices, OS versions, and network types without costly infrastructure.
- Allows for parallel testing to optimize workflows and handle diverse app demands.

Overcoming 2025 Mobile Testing Challenges

Future challenges like rising cybersecurity risks, cross-device compatibility, and growing automation demands will test QA teams. Address these issues by:

4. **Embedding CI/CD Pipelines:** Deliver fast updates without sacrificing quality.
