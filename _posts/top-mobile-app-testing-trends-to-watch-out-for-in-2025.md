Testing apps in simulated environments—like fluctuating network signals or battery drain—is vital for ensuring dependability under challenging conditions. Platforms like Zof AI are helping QA teams identify vulnerabilities and improve app resilience.

### Privacy-First Testing & Security Enhancements
As users demand better data protection, privacy-first methodologies will become central to app testing strategies. AI-driven security tests and privacy-by-design approaches are paving the way for safer, GDPR-compliant applications.
