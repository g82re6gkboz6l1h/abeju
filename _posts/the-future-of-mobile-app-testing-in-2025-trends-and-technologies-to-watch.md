## The Evolution of Mobile Testing in 2025

Mobile testing has expanded significantly from simple functionality checks to ensuring exceptional usability, security, and adaptability across technologies like foldable screens and IoT devices. Here's how QA professionals will need to advance into 2025:

3. **5G and Network Testing Beyond**: Validate apps for cutting-edge bandwidth scenarios.
4. **Device Diversity**: Ensure apps adapt to countless devices ranging from foldable phones to smart wearables.
5. **Heightened Security Needs**: Perform robust privacy and cybersecurity checks to combat future threats.
