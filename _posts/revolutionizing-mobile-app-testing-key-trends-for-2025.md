## Shift-Left Testing: Integrating Quality from the Start

Shift-left testing embeds testing in the early stages of app development to reduce costs, enable early bug detection, and shorten release cycles. With AI tools like Zof AI, testers can automate unit testing and integrate continuous testing workflows into Agile and DevOps frameworks.
