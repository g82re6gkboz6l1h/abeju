## Key Testing Challenges for Foldable Devices

- **Screen State Transitions:** Validating apps for folding/unfolding layouts.
- **UI/UX Adapting to Dual Displays:** Ensuring consistent user experience across split/multi-screen setups.
- **Hardware Variability:** Coping with diverse hinge mechanisms and resolutions.
- **Battery Optimization:** Testing performance efficiency across power-intensive use cases.
