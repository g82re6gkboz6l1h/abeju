## How AI Is Transforming QA Testing

In traditional QA testing, manual effort limited scalability and efficiency, even as automation entered the picture. The inclusion of AI reshapes these dynamics by introducing intelligence-driven improvements:

1. **Self-Learning Algorithms**: Continuous improvement through analyzing past test scenarios and outcomes.
2. **Faster Release Cycles**: Automated bug detection accelerates testing timelines.
3. **Predictive Analytics**: Anticipating bugs and app vulnerabilities before deployment.
4. **Enhanced Test Coverage**: AI can simulate real-world conditions across devices, OS versions, and networks.

AI-driven QA testing is rapidly becoming integral for modern mobile app development processes.
