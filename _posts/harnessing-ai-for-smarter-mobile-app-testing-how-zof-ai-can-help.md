Mobile app testing has evolved drastically from labor-intensive manual testing to semi-automated solutions. Here's a quick timeline:

- **AI-Driven Testing:** Modern AI tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) autonomously predict bugs, adapt to testing scenarios, and improve their results based on data patterns.

AI has created a significant leap forward, enabling QA systems to analyze, adapt, and optimize with minimal human intervention.
