Today's app testing is shifting from manual methods to AI-driven automation. Manual testing can be slow and prone to human error. AI and ML solve these issues by analyzing extensive data sets, predicting potential flaws, and automating repetitive tasks.

For example, ML algorithms can detect recurring defects based on historical test data, allowing developers to optimize future testing strategies. Similarly, AI-powered tools uncover hidden vulnerabilities, providing actionable insights to strengthen app performance.
