## Key IoT and Wearable Testing Strategies
Testing wearable apps requires rigorous frameworks tailored to dynamic IoT environments. Here are essential strategies to follow:

### 1. **Device Compatibility Testing**
Ensure seamless communication across diverse devices and protocols like Bluetooth, Zigbee, or Wi-Fi.

### 2. **Security and Privacy Testing**
Rigorous encryption mechanisms and secure data transmission protocols protect sensitive user information.

Applications must handle bandwidth variations, signal interruptions, and real-world connectivity challenges effortlessly.
