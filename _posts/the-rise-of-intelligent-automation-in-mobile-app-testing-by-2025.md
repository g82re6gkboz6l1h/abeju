## Understanding Intelligent Automation in Mobile App Testing

Intelligent automation integrates AI, machine learning, and smart algorithms with automated workflows, enabling systems to perform advanced tasks with minimal human intervention. In the context of QA, it has the potential to:

- Adapt to app updates and rapidly fix test scripts.
- Predict vulnerabilities leveraging historical data.
- Automate exploratory tests for hidden issues.
- Provide extensive test coverage, including overlooked edge cases.

By prioritizing efficiency and precision, intelligent automation helps testers shift their focus from mundane tasks to high-value activities like analyzing insights and enhancing user experience.
