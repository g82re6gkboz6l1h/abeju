## Why Traditional Mobile App Testing Methods Are Falling Short

### Challenges Faced by Developers

1. **Manual Testing Inefficiencies**: Time-consuming and prone to human errors.
2. **Automation Limitations**: Requires extensive coding expertise, struggles to adapt to evolving app environments.
3. **Device Fragmentation**: Testing across thousands of mobile devices and OS configurations is nearly impossible manually.
4. **Frequent Updates**: Agile cycles demand constant retesting of apps.
5. **Complex User Journeys**: Intricate workflows like payment gateways and personalized content amplify testing challenges.

To address these issues, developers are moving toward smarter, AI-driven solutions.
