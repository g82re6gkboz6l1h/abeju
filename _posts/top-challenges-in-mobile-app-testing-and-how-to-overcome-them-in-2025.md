## Key Challenges in Mobile App Testing
Discover the top obstacles shaping mobile app testing in 2025:

1. **Device Fragmentation**: Apps need to function seamlessly across countless smartphone models, diverse screen sizes, and versions of operating systems like Android and iOS.
2. **Frequent OS Updates**: Regular updates introduce new functionalities requiring apps to stay compatible without dropping support for older systems.
3. **Network Variability**: Apps must work efficiently on 5G, 4G, and fluctuating low-bandwidth connections, making simulation complex yet critical.
4. **Performance Optimization**: Users prioritize apps that load fast, operate smoothly, and conserve battery life.
5. **Data Privacy & Security**: Data-heavy apps must comply with regulations like GDPR and CCPA while safeguarding against breaches.
