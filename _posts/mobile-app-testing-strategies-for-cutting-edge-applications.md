### Proven Strategies for Mobile App Testing
- **Device Fragmentation Testing**: Comprehensive cross-device compatibility testing using physical devices and emulators.
- **Manual vs. Automated Testing**: Balancing manual and automated approaches for exploratory and repetitive test scenarios.
- **Network Condition Testing**: Simulate real-world environments like low bandwidth, 3G/5G, and disconnections.
- **Performance Testing**: Track key metrics including load time, battery usage, and responsiveness.
- **Security Testing**: Identify vulnerabilities and bolster user data protection.
- **Cross-Platform Testing**: Ensure seamless app features and functionality for iOS and Android users.
- **Continuous Testing Integration**: Incorporate testing into CI/CD pipelines for faster development cycles.
