## Why Mobile App Testing is Changing
Mobile app testing is shifting from simple functionality checks to delivering exceptional user experiences. Drivers include:

- Growing **device diversity** (wearables, foldable phones, IoT).
- Increasing complexity with features like AR/VR, streaming, and AI.
- Demands for faster app updates without sacrificing quality.
- **Global accessibility** for users worldwide.

By 2025, testing will emphasize end-user satisfaction and adaptability, and businesses must adopt innovative strategies to thrive.
