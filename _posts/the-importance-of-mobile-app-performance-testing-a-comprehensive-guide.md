## Essential Performance Metrics Developers Must Monitor

### 1. **Response Time**
   Minimize lag to enhance user experience.
### 2. **App Load Time**
   Ensure loading times are under 3 seconds to maintain user engagement.
### 3. **Crash Rate**
   Limit app crashes to build user confidence.
### 4. **Memory & Battery Usage**
   Optimize RAM and battery drain for better app performance.
### 5. **Scalability**
   Test handling of multiple concurrent users without affecting speed.
