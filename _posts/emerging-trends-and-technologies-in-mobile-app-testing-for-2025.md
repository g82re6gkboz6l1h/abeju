## Role of AI and ML in Automating Testing Processes

Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning redefine mobile app testing, introducing smarter, automated workflows:

1. **Test Automation:** AI builds adaptive scripts, reducing manual effort and enabling dynamic test case generation.
2. **Defect Prediction:** Machine learning algorithms predict vulnerable areas in coding, enabling focused error diagnostics.
3. **Visual Testing Tools:** AI visual checkers streamline testing across varying screen resolutions and device formats.
4. **Continuous Testing:** ML algorithms analyze real-time datasets for seamless iterative improvement.

Importance of Testing Apps for Foldable and Multi-Screen Devices

With foldable phones and multi-screen gadgets thriving in 2025, testers face challenges ensuring apps perform seamlessly:

- **Screen State Transitions:** Apps must adapt as users shift between folded and expanded screens.
- **Aspect Ratios:** Unconventional screen sizes demand custom UI/UX testing practices.
- **Multi-App Environments:** Testers must validate app behavior across simultaneous usage scenarios.
