## Proven Mobile App Testing Strategies

1. **Shift-Left Testing for Early Detection**: Catch bugs early during development to save time and resources.
2. **Functional Testing**: Examine if your app functions correctly, especially core features like login and transactions.
3. **Performance Testing**: Test under various conditions to ensure speed, responsiveness, and stability.
4. **Usability Testing**: Replicate real-world usage to check app intuitiveness and user-friendly design.
5. **Automation Testing**: Use automated tools for repetitive tests to increase efficiency.
6. **Security Testing**: Identify and resolve vulnerabilities to protect user data.
