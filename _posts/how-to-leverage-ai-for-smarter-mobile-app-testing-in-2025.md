3. **Broader & Deeper Test Coverage**
   AI ensures the most complex user interactions and edge cases are tested efficiently, elevating the app's quality.

4. **Faster Testing Execution**
   Intelligent automation optimizes the testing process, reducing time-to-market significantly.
