## Key Features to Prioritize in Testing Tools

When choosing mobile app testing software, focus on features that enhance quality assurance, streamline workflows, and accommodate your project’s scale:

- **Automation**: Automate repetitive tests for faster execution and consistency.

- **Cloud-Based Testing**: Deploy scalable testing environments without hardware limitations.
