### Key Challenges in Mobile App Testing

Mobile app testing involves factors such as:

- **Device Fragmentation:** Apps must perform well on an array of devices.
- **Network Conditions:** Testing across varying bandwidths impacts app responsiveness.
- **Balancing Manual and Automated Testing:** Accurate balance ensures efficiency without imposing limitations.
- **Meeting User Standards:** Users expect intuitive design and flawless functionality.

To overcome these challenges, selecting the right testing tools is essential.
