## 1. Key Challenges Facing Mobile App Testing

### Increasing Complexity of Modern Applications
Mobile apps now integrate AI, IoT, and AR technologies, enhancing the user experience but complicating the testing process. Teams must test apps thoroughly under various scenarios.

### Rising Expectations for Performance & Usability
With users demanding flawless apps with fast speeds and intuitive designs, meeting these standards requires rigorous testing to avoid negative reviews and revenue loss.

### Device & OS Fragmentation
The ever-growing diversity of devices and operating systems, including custom environments like Xiaomi MIUI, demands extensive compatibility testing to ensure apps run smoothly across thousands of configurations.

### Real-Time Updates & Continuous Development
DevOps and Agile practices require developers to deliver frequent updates, making comprehensive testing critical to upholding app quality standards.
