## AI-Powered Predictive Analytics in Testing

Predictive analytics brings foresight into QA workflows, highlighting defects in their nascent stages. AI studies historical data, performance issues, and user patterns to predict app vulnerabilities before they escalate.

For instance, AI tools can identify anomalies such as server overloads during high usage, ensuring such issues never reach end users. This proactive approach builds confidence in app releases and minimizes user-reported errors.
