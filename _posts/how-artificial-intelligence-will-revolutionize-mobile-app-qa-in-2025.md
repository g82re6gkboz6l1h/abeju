## Why AI is Reshaping Software Testing

The conventional manual approach to software testing involves tedious processes that are time-consuming and prone to human error. Even existing automated solutions often fall short due to static scripts requiring frequent changes. AI, powered by technologies like machine learning, offers a smarter alternative by constantly learning from data and adapting to ecosystem updates. AI tools bring:

- Rapid bug detection.
- Self-healing test scripts.
- Adaptive, real-time testing processes.
- Unparalleled scalability for complex applications.

By 2025, these advancements will be key in releasing superior mobile applications faster.
