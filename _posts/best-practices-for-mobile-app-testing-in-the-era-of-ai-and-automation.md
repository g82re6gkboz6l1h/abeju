## Building a Balanced Approach: Manual vs Automated Testing

Striking the right balance between manual and automated testing is vital for comprehensive quality assurance.

### Recommended Practices:
- **Manual Testing**: Focus on usability and UX elements that require human judgment.
- **Automated Testing**: Ideal for repetitive tasks like regression and performance benchmarking. Tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai) assist in complex scenario simulations.

Adopt a hybrid approach to leverage the best of both worlds.
