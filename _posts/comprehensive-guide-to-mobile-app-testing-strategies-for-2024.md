## Why Mobile App Testing Is Crucial in 2024

Mobile technology's growth heightens the need for testing:
- **Expanding Device & OS Ecosystems:** Adapting apps to frequent updates and diverse device specifications.
- **Demand for Seamless User Experience**: Prevent crashes and bugs to promote user satisfaction.
- **Rising Security Threats**: Strengthen data protections against cyberattacks.
- **Competitive Market**: Ensure superior app performance to stand out.

---

Essential Testing Phases Explained

- **Functional Testing**: Verifies features' correct functionality (e.g., login processes, notifications).
- **Usability Testing**: Evaluates design and navigation from the user's perspective.
- **Security Testing**: Identifies vulnerabilities and protects user data.
