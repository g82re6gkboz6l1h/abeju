## Key Challenges Facing Mobile App Testing in 2025

The landscape of mobile technology in 2025 is expected to introduce fresh challenges for app testing, including:

1. **Impact of 5G**: Mobile apps will need robust testing to operate seamlessly under varying 5G-enabled speeds.

2. **Rise of Foldable Devices & Wearables**: These unique form factors create new compatibility challenges for testers.

3. **Global Accessibility**: Localization, regional compliance, and cultural needs demand thorough insights during testing.

4. **IoT Devices Integration**: Mobile apps integrated with IoT must undergo extensive tests to ensure interconnected functionality.
