## How Mobile App Testing Has Evolved

From manual testing in early mobile apps to AI-driven automation, the field has continuously evolved. Early testing was hands-on but couldn’t scale with increasingly complex apps. Automated testing revolutionized this by offering faster and more reliable execution.

By 2025, testing methodologies will advance further. Key drivers include AI-powered automation leading predictive maintenance and hybrid approaches blending machine learning with human oversight. Factors like IoT integration, AR/VR, and 5G connectivity will demand scaled testing environments to meet future technology standards.
