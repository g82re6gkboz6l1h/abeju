Ensure Multi-Device Compatibility
Tests must address a fragmented mobile landscape with foldables, wearables, smart devices, and more:
- **Dynamic UI Testing**: Adapt layouts for varied screen types and resolutions.
- **Cloud Platforms**: Cut down physical testing hurdles using virtual device libraries.

Strengthen Security Against Advanced Threats
Cyber threats in 2025 will be more complex and pervasive. Prioritize robust security testing:
- **Encryption Protocols**: Secure sensitive data storage and transmission.
- **Authentication Checks**: Test biometrics and multi-factor methods.
- **Penetration Testing**: Employ AI tools to identify vulnerabilities and predict attack vectors.
