Manual vs. Automated Testing in 2025

### Manual Testing Benefits:
- Excels in exploratory testing and assessing usability or UX nuances.
- Allows human testers to identify unexpected behaviors.

Drawbacks:
- Time-consuming, error-prone, and less scalable compared to automated solutions.

### Automated Testing Benefits:
- Ideal for repetitive, regression, and large-scale tests.
- AI-driven platforms like Zof AI reduce testing time with smarter workflows.
- Continuous testing supports Agile and DevOps pipelines.

Drawback:
- High initial setup costs and lacks the intuition of manual approaches for UX testing.

In 2025, a hybrid approach combining manual oversight with automated precision is most effective. AI supports this balance by bridging gaps in traditional testing strategies.
