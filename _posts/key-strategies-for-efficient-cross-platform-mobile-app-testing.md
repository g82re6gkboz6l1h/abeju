## What is Cross-Platform Mobile App Testing?

Cross-platform mobile app testing evaluates applications for performance, functionality, and user experience across multiple platforms, including Android, iOS, and Windows. Testing ensures compatibility across various devices, screen sizes, OS versions, and environments—crucial for apps built with React Native, Flutter, or Xamarin frameworks.
