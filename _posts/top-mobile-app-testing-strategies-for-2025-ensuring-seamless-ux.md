## Major Challenges in Mobile App Testing for 2025

1. **Device Fragmentation**: With countless devices and operating systems, ensuring compatibility remains a hurdle.
2. **Accelerated Development Cycles**: Agile and DevOps methodologies demand rapid testing during continuous delivery.
3. **Highly Complex Features**: Testing for AI-enhanced and cloud-connected features necessitates advanced strategies.
4. **Mounting Security Concerns**: Continuous threats like data breaches require rigorous security checks.
5. **High User Expectations**: Apps must deliver seamless experiences to retain users.
6. **Effective AI Integration**: Teams must efficiently implement AI tools like Zof AI to avoid creating additional challenges.
