## Types of Testing Crucial for Mobile Apps
To achieve robust app quality, multiple testing methods are required to address every potential issue. Here are key mobile app testing types:

### 1. Functional Testing
Verifies functionality and alignment with app goals.

### 2. Performance Testing
Evaluates responsiveness and load-handling under stress and normal conditions.

### 3. Usability Testing
Assesses ease of use, accessibility, and navigation for users.

### 4. Security Testing
Identifies vulnerabilities to avoid data breaches.

### 5. Compatibility Testing
Ensures functionality across devices, OS versions, and configurations.

### 6. Regression Testing
Validates previous functionalities after updates to ensure nothing is broken.

### 7. Localization Testing
Confirms app effectiveness for different languages, currencies, and cultural nuances.

### 8. Automated Testing
Speeds up repetitive operations through testing frameworks.

Pitfalls to Avoid While Testing
A few oversights can derail your testing strategies, even with a well-prepared QA team.

### Major Testing Pitfalls:
1. **Unclear Objectives:** Undefined KPIs lead to poor problem targeting.
2. **Limited Test Coverage:** Neglecting usability or security tests creates gaps in competence.
3. **Overlooking Real Situations:** Testing only on lab models misses real-world factors like network variability.
4. **Bypassing Regression Checks:** Not verifying updates leads to missed issues.
5. **Over-Reliance on Manual Testing:** Ignoring automation strategies drains time and reduces accuracy.
