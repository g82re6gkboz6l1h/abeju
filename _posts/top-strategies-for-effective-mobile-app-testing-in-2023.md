## Key Challenges During Mobile App Testing
1. **Device Fragmentation**: Testing across thousands of devices and diverse configurations.
2. **OS Updates**: Frequent updates impacting app compatibility.
3. **Network Variability**: Handling app performance in slow or offline environments.
4. **Security Concerns**: Data safety issues in sensitive apps.
5. **Evolving User Expectations**: Continual optimization to meet shifting tastes.
6. **Limited Resources**: Small teams struggling with extensive testing.
