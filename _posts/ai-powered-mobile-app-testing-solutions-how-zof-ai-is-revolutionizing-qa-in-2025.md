AI-driven testing minimizes human errors during tight deadlines, providing more precise results through automated procedures.

### 4. **Dynamic Adaptability**
AI’s self-learning capabilities ensure smooth adaptation to changing app designs and functionalities.

### 5. **Cost Efficiency**
Automating QA processes with AI reduces operational costs, saving time and resources.
