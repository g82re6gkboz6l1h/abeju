## Comprehensive Types of Mobile App Testing

Mobile app testing encompasses various approaches to scrutinize different aspects of app performance, quality, and user satisfaction.

### **1. Functional Testing**
Verify app features and workflows function as intended.
- **Example:** Testing login functionality to ensure correct credentials proceed.

### **2. Performance Testing**
Assess speed, responsiveness, and resource consumption in varied conditions.
- **Example:** Testing app stability during high traffic scenarios.

### **3. Compatibility Testing**
Ensure seamless operation across devices, screen sizes, and OS variations.
- **Example:** Test app performance on Android and iOS versions.

### **4. Usability Testing**
Enhance interface simplicity, design, and navigation.
- **Example:** Optimize task completion time, such as purchases or searches.

### **5. Security Testing**
Prevent data breaches and vulnerabilities.
- **Example:** Verify user data encryption.

### **6. Localization Testing**
Tailor app experiences to meet global standards.
- **Example:** Adapt pricing and language for diverse markets.
