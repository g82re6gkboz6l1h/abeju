4. Cross-Platform Compatibility Best Practices 

Adapting to diverse mobile OS and device ecosystems in 2025 is key:
- **Device Fragmentation**: Automate tests across various screen sizes, hardware specs, and OS versions.
- **Responsive Design**: Optimize for foldable devices, wearables, and hybrid technologies.
- **Platform-Specific Testing**: Detect bugs unique to Android/iOS and align with native features.
- **API Testing**: Ensure consistent performance across devices using third-party APIs.

Comprehensive compatibility checks ensure apps work seamlessly across platforms.
