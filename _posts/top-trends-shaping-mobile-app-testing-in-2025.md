Foldable and Wearable Device Compatibility Testing

Foldable screens and wearables are reshaping app development. Testers need innovative frameworks to ensure seamless functionality across devices.

**Foldable Devices:**
- Handle multi-window transitions.
- Adapt screen overlays for different orientations.

**Wearables:**
- Optimize user interfaces for smaller screens.
- Validate sensor-based functionalities, such as health trackers.

As these devices grow, compatibility testing will be a crucial focus.
