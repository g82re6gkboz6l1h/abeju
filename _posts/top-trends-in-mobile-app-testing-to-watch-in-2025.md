### 2. Rise of Hyper-Automation Testing Techniques
Hyper-automation, which broadens traditional automation, will dominate. Techniques leveraging robotic process automation (RPA) and AI will reduce repetitive tasks and speed delivery cycles.

### 3. Specialized AR/VR Testing Environments
As AR and VR apps see increased adoption, testers are challenged to evaluate performance, graphics quality, and interactivity. Advanced tools ensure immersive technology aligns flawlessly with user expectations.

### 4. Cloud and Edge Computing Impact
Both cloud-based solutions and edge computing are critical for data synchronization, high-speed responsiveness, and decentralized testing methodologies.

### 5. Security as a Core Priority
With mobile platforms handling ever-growing volumes of sensitive data, security testing innovations are crucial. Enhanced analysis of biometric systems and AI-assisted threat modeling will take center stage.

### 6. Continuous Testing Within Agile and DevOps
Continuous testing will feature heavily in development pipelines by integrating testing at all stages to perfect app delivery.
