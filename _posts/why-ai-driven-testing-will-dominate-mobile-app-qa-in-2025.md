## What Is AI-Driven Testing?

AI-driven testing leverages advanced machine learning algorithms to optimize the entire QA process. This includes automating repetitive tasks such as: 
- Test creation 
- Performance analysis 
- Real-time issue detection.

Unlike traditional manual testing, AI focuses on predictive analytics and continuous improvement, making it indispensable as mobile applications become more complex with multi-platform compatibility and personalized user experiences.
