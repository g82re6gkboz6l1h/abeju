## Introduction to Mobile App Testing Evolution

The evolution of mobile app testing reflects groundbreaking changes over time. Initially dominated by manual methods, QA teams struggled to test apps across diverse devices and ecosystems. Automation tools like Selenium, Appium, and XCUITest later revolutionized this process, enabling scalability and quicker testing cycles. Still, 2025's challenges call for even more sophisticated approaches, such as integrating AI, smart devices, and dynamic interfaces into the testing process.
