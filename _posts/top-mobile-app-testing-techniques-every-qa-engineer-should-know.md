## Manual vs. Automated Testing: Pros & Cons

**Manual Testing Advantages**:
- Delivers in-depth user experience insights.
- Best for exploratory and UI/UX testing.

**Automated Testing Advantages**:
- Faster execution and scalability.
- Great for regression and performance testing.

Combine both methods to maximize app testing efficiency.
