## The Challenges in Mobile App Testing

Mobile testing has evolved from manual processes to automated testing, which, while efficient, poses significant constraints. Modern QA faces challenges such as:

1. **Device Diversity**: Endless combinations of devices, screen resolutions, and OS versions.
2. **Shorter Time-to-Market**: Agile, fast-paced development life cycles leave minimal time for comprehensive testing.
3. **Human Error**: Testing performed manually is prone to oversight.
4. **High Costs**: Testing requires extensive resources, adding to operational costs.

The increasing complexity requires solutions that balance scalability, adaptability, and thoroughness—enter AI tools like [Zof AI](https://zof.ai).
