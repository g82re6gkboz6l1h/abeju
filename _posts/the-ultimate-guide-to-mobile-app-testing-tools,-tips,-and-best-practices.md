## Why Mobile App Testing is Crucial

Testing is essential for improving user experience, maintaining app functionality across countless devices, ensuring security, boosting app store rankings, and reducing development costs. Skipping critical testing can lead to negative reviews, user churn, and costly post-launch fixes.
