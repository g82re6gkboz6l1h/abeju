## Traditional vs. Next-Gen Testing Tools
Traditional tools like Selenium and Appium rely heavily on manual processes and pre-written scripts. While they automate repetitive tasks, they struggle with dynamic updates and real-world conditions. In contrast, next-gen solutions excel with **adaptive capabilities**, self-healing mechanisms, and predictive analysis, eliminating time-intensive scripting and enhancing efficiency. Tools like Zof AI are paving the way for **scalable**, contextual, and precise testing solutions.
