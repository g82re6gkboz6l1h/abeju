5G Networks and IoT: Challenging App Testing

### Mobile Testing in a 5G Ecosystem
With enhanced speed and reduced latency, 5G introduces higher data loads. Testing strategies will evolve to simulate real-time connectivity scenarios on varied network conditions.

### IoT: Scalability Concerns
From smart homes to industrial IoT systems, apps rely on large networks of devices. QA testers will shift focus to interoperability tests across platforms and protocols, with tools optimizing data exchange processes for smoother IoT workflows.
