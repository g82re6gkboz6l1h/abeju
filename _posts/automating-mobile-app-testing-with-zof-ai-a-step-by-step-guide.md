## Why Mobile App Testing Needs Automation

Mobile app testing ensures reliability across OS versions, devices, and screen sizes. However, manual testing struggles to meet ever-increasing complexities. Why should developers shift their focus to automation?

### Key Challenges with Manual Testing:

#### 1. **Time-Consuming Repetitions**  
Manual testing involves repetitive, labor-intensive tasks, eating into developers’ valuable time. Automation solves this inefficiency by completing large-scale tests in less time.

#### 2. **Prone to Human Errors**  
Even seasoned testers miss bugs when managing complicated flows. Automation eliminates inconsistencies, ensuring accuracy for every test cycle.

#### 3. **Limited Scalability**  
As apps evolve and testing environments grow, scaling manual testing is impractical. Automation can handle extensive scalability demands.

#### 4. **Resource Limitations**  
Testing teams get overburdened with mundane tasks. Automated workflows free them to focus on enhancing app performance and design.
